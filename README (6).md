# Digital-Literacy
This portfolio serves as a strategic roadmap for students to transition from casual internet 
users to professional digital citizens. In an era where a "digital footprint" is as important as a 
GPA, this project provides the tools to build, collaborate, and protect one’s identity in the 
modern workforce.
Creating a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic : creating a one-page visual resource that 
helps batchmates understand what Digital Literacy means and why it matters
Building Your Student Digital Portfolio : setting up the foundational portfolio across the key 
platforms that employers, internship coordinators, and the academic community check
Explore Coding & Collaboration Platforms : right online tools for studying, practicing, and 
collaborating. This task builds familiarity with two categories: coding practice platforms and 
cloud collaboration tools
Professional Email & Etiquette Guide :. This task tests whether you can apply email etiquette 
yourself, and whether you can communicate those rules clearly to others.
Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide : helping your peers recognize and 
avoid online threats. This task requires to research a real or realistic cybercrime scenario 
and produce an awareness resource.
